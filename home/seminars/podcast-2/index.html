Knot Project Space Podcast 2

In this second episode of Knot Project Space’s new podcast series, we meet with Maize Longboat to talk about what it was like to make ‘Terra Nova’; and Ashley Bowa and Lesley Marshall talk about Green Gazing, a multi-experiential work composed of an immersive and interactive installation, a meditative movement session and a community discussion.in Space Grey is a durational mostly a-synchronous online exhibition meditating on themes of connection, environmental extraction and accelerated capitalism.

Presenting works by Ashley Bowa & Lesley Marshall, WhiteFeather Hunter, Maize Longboat, Tina Pearson, Manuel Piña-Baldoquín, Emilio Portal and Tosca Teran.
